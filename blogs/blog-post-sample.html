Starting My Developer Journey

November 16, 2025 • 5 min read
Journey Introduction AI

Today marks a significant milestone in my life. I'm officially embarking on a journey to become a professional software developer. In an era where artificial intelligence dominates headlines and sparks debates about the future of work, many people ask me: "Why now? Why coding?"

The Question Everyone Asks

"Isn't AI going to replace all the developers?" It's a fair question, one I've asked myself countless times. But here's what I believe: AI is a tool, not a replacement. Just as calculators didn't eliminate mathematicians, AI won't eliminate developers—it will change what it means to be one.

"The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now." - Chinese Proverb

Why I'm Documenting This Journey

I want to create a transparent, honest chronicle of what it's really like to break into software development in 2025. No sugar-coating, no overnight success stories—just the real journey with all its ups and downs.

What You Can Expect

  • Weekly Updates: Progress reports on what I'm learning
  • Honest Reflections: The challenges, frustrations, and breakthroughs
  • Resources: Everything I find useful along the way
  • AI Integration: How I'm using AI tools as learning aids

The Road Ahead

My plan is straightforward but ambitious:

  1. Master the fundamentals (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  2. Build real projects that solve actual problems
  3. Learn a backend language (leaning toward Python)
  4. Create a portfolio that showcases my skills
  5. Land my first developer job

I know it won't be easy. I know there will be moments of doubt. But I also know that every expert was once a beginner. This blog—and my YouTube channel—will document every step of that transformation.

Join Me

Whether you're considering a similar journey, already coding, or just curious about what it takes to become a developer in the AI age, I hope you'll follow along. Let's prove that with determination, the right resources, and yes—even AI as a learning tool—it's absolutely possible to break into tech in 2025.

Here's to new beginnings, endless debugging sessions, and hopefully, a successful career transition. Let's see where this journey takes us.
